美国多个州的总检察长联合提交了一份法庭意见书，支持阻止特朗普提议的凯旋门建设。特朗普上周宣布希望加快这一项目的进程。这一事件引发了广泛关注，因为凯旋门的建设可能会对当地社区和环境产生深远影响。法律挑战的结果可能会影响未来类似项目的审批流程，尤其是在涉及公共利益和环境保护的情况下。
English
A coalition of state attorneys general has filed an amicus brief in support of halting the construction of Trump's proposed triumphal arch. Last week, Trump announced his intention to fast-track the project. This situation has garnered significant attention, as the construction of the arch could have profound effects on the local community and environment. The outcome of this legal challenge may influence the approval processes for similar projects in the future, particularly those involving public interest and environmental protection.
