English
Chilean architect Smiljan Radic has been named the next Pritzker Prize laureate, a decision that has garnered significant attention. The announcement was delayed due to the revelation of Jeffrey Epstein's friendship with Tom Pritzker, who has since distanced himself from the award. This incident not only affects the reputation of the Pritzker Prize but also sparks discussions about ethical standards in the architecture community, potentially influencing future selections and public perceptions of architects.
