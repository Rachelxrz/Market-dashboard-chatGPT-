10. Construction material costs set to rise if Strait of Hormuz blockade continues
来源：Dezeen ｜ 发布时间：2026-03-25 10:30 UTC
中文
霍尔木兹海峡的持续封锁可能导致建筑材料价格上涨，包括钢铁和水泥。全球建筑咨询公司Linesight的研究警告称，如果这一水道的干扰持续，建筑行业需为更高的材料成本做好准备。这一情况不仅会影响建筑项目的预算，还可能导致整体建筑市场的波动，进而影响经济发展。
English
The ongoing blockade of the Strait of Hormuz could lead to rising prices for construction materials, such as steel and cement. A study by global construction consultant Linesight warns that if disruptions continue in this critical waterway, the construction industry should brace for increased costs. This situation could not only affect project budgets but also lead to fluctuations in the overall construction market, potentially impacting economic growth.
