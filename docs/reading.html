10. ‘Pressure’ Review: The Weather of War
来源：NYT > Arts ｜ 发布时间：2026-05-28 15:31 UTC
中文
电影《压力》以第二次世界大战为背景，聚焦于诺曼底登陆前夕的天气报告对战局的影响。尽管影片的情节设定在战争的混乱中，但其叙述却显得平淡无奇。这部电影的重要性在于，它揭示了天气条件如何在战争中扮演关键角色，影响着军事决策和士兵的命运。这样的主题不仅反映了历史的真实一面，也可能引发观众对战争中细节的重新思考。
English
The film "Pressure" is set against the backdrop of World War II, focusing on how weather reports leading up to D-Day influenced military strategy. Despite its setting in the chaos of war, the narrative feels rather dull. The significance of this film lies in its exploration of how weather conditions play a crucial role in warfare, affecting military decisions and the fates of soldiers. This theme not only reflects a true aspect of history but may also prompt viewers to reconsider the importance of details in times of conflict.
